About EAGLE Token

EAGLE is a cutting-edge Web3 utility token built on the TON blockchain designed to empower traders with premium trading signals, staking rewards, and governance capabilities. With a total supply of 1 billion tokens, EAGLE aims to revolutionize crypto signal platforms by combining decentralized finance, community governance, and secure, transparent tokenomics.

Holders of EAGLE token gain exclusive access to advanced trading tools, a private Telegram signal group, and upcoming staking and DAO features. Supported by trusted partners like Blum.io, STON.fi, and the TON ecosystem, EAGLE is positioned as a forward-thinking project bridging DeFi and real trading utility.
